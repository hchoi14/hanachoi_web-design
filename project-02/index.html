Variables, Conditionals plus Chance Operations Poster

Medium: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, acrylic paint, and random dice generator.

The process of generative image making is based upon a system of conditions, variables, and parameters that can alter the image through a successive series of permutations. This project uses different conditions such as looping, sequence length, and placement plus variable changes of patterns, scanned paint, photos, and photomontages.This experiment lead to final 4 poster designs.

Korean Snack Shirts

Medium: Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop

A Shirt designs for the “Fam” (Family group) under Korean American Student Associations. Inspired by popular Korean snacks: Pepero = cookie stick that is dipped in chocolate Melona = Korean ice pop with a melon flavor Bingsu = Korean shaved ice dessert with different toppings you can choose to put it on top Each Fam has a logo and shirt design.
